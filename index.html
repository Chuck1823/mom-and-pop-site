Switching over

Moving from your current booking system

Email your customer list, upcoming appointments, and service menu to your dedicated Desk inbox. We load everything during setup.

  1. Export: Download your data from Square, Fresha, Booksy, etc.
  2. Email it: Send it to your Desk's email address.
  3. We load it in: We enter everything during the 30-minute setup.
  4. Parallel run: We keep your old system active for the first week.
  5. Cancel the old one: Once you see it working, you're done.
